Advik Metals and Minerals is a Canadian-based global mining and trading company dedicated to supplying premium scrap metals, copper ore, and industrial raw materials such as coal, iron ore, aluminum, zinc, and lead.

With strategically located mines in Colombia, Peru, Chile, and Ecuador , we operate as a vertically integrated supplier, controlling every step from extraction to export. This ensures consistent quality, competitive pricing, and on-time delivery—every time.

We work directly with refineries, smelters, and large-scale industrial buyers, providing bulk shipments backed by transparent trade practices and reliable logistics.

Copper Ore

Advik Metals and Minerals specializes in the export of high-grade copper ore suitable for smelting and refining applications. Each batch is independently verified to ensure consistent metal content and purity.

Copper (Cu) 8% – 25%
Moisture <10%
Form Crushed / Bulk Ore
Certified By SGS / Intertek
Applications: Refining, smelting, and manufacturing of electrical wiring, alloys, and industrial components.

Coal

Advik Metals and Minerals supplies both thermal coal and coking coal for industrial and energy production use. Our coal products are sourced from reliable mining partners, ensuring consistent calorific value and low impurity levels.

Types

  • Thermal Coal (power generation)
  • Coking Coal (steelmaking & industrial furnaces)

Specifications

Calorific Value 5,000 – 7,000 kcal/kg
Ash <12%
Sulfur <1%
Moisture <10%
Applications: Power generation, metallurgy, steel manufacturing, and cement production.
